Welcome to Live Therapy session

When you click on the "Start" button, the music therapy session would begin and the music would start playing. If you click on the "Pause" button, the music would stop playing and the timer would pause. This would allow you to take a break from the therapy session or pause it for any other reason. When you're ready to resume the session, you could click the "Pause" button again to start the music and timer back up.

 
 



 

Merits And Demerits

Merits of Relaxation Music

  • Helps reduce stress and anxiety
  • Can improve mood and overall well-being
  • May aid in better sleep

Demerits of Relaxation Music

  • May not be effective for everyone
  • Can be distracting for some people
  • Some people may find it boring or unappealing
